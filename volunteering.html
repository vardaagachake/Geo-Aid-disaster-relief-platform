Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register?
Simply fill out the form above and our team will get in touch with you within 48 hours.
Is there any training required?
Yes, we provide comprehensive online training modules for all volunteers to ensure you're well-prepared.
Can I volunteer remotely?
Yes, some roles like data analysis and social media advocacy can be done remotely from anywhere.
How much time do I need to commit?
It depends on your availability. We have options for full-time, part-time, and occasional volunteers.
