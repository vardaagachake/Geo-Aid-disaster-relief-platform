What We Do

Geo-Aid leverages advanced technology to provide real-time disaster-related information across India. Our mission is to empower communities and response teams with accurate, up-to-date insights that help in making quick and informed decisions during emergencies.

We utilize satellite data, crowd-sourced reports, and AI-driven analysis to create detailed maps and impact assessments. By integrating various data sources, we deliver comprehensive situational awareness, helping authorities and relief organizations prioritize their efforts efficiently.

Our platform covers a wide range of disaster scenarios, including floods, cyclones, earthquakes, and landslides. We focus on delivering information through intuitive dashboards and mobile apps, making critical data accessible to both decision-makers and the public.

With no reliance on hardware and a purely information-driven approach, Geo-Aid remains agile and cost-effective. By bridging the gap between data and action, we enhance disaster response capabilities, ensuring faster and more coordinated relief efforts across the nation.
